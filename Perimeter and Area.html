Perimeter and Area

Perimeter

The perimeter is the length of the outline of a shape.Perimeter is measured in units such as millimetres, centimetres and metres.

Area

The area is the space occupied by a flat shape or the surface of an object. The area of a figure is the number of unit squares that cover the surface of a closed figure. Area is measured in square units such as square centimteres, square feet, square inches, etc.

Square

Perimeter: 4*Side

Area: Side*Side

Rectangle

Perimeter: 2(length+breadth)

Area: length*breadth

Circle

Circumference: 2 πradius

Area: πradius2

Parallelogram

Perimeter: 2a+2b

Area: breadth*height

Cuboid

Perimeter: 2ab+2ac+2bc

Volume: abc

Cube

Area: 6*length2

Volume: length3
